About Us

Colleen Anne Zalamea, or 'Colleen,' is a third-year FEU Alabang student with a passion for baking and creating DIY goods. One of her favorite hobbies is crafting beautiful DIY flowers, a talent she is turning into a blossoming business.

Each Valentine’s Day, she offers a range of uniquely designed flowers in various colors, perfect for couples and anyone looking to gift their special someone something truly meaningful.

Made with love and creativity, Colleen’s flowers are a heartfelt way to express affection. You can find her selling her charming creations at UG in FEU Alabang, and she’s soon expanding her reach by promoting her products online!
